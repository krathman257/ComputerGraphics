# Maze Controls

The Stone Pillar marks the beginning of the maze
The Stone Icosphere marks the end of the maze

* SPACE - Switch between Bird's-Eye view and Camera view
* ESCAPE - Close window

# Bird's-Eye Controls

* W - Move Up
* S - Move Down
* A - Move Left
* D - Move Right

# Camera Controls

* W - Move Forward
* S - Move Backward
* A - Rotate Left
* D - Rotate Right
* Q - Strafe Left
* E - Strafe Right
* R - Look Up
* F - Look Down
